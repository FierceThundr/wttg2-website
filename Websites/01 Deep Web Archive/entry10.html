You are one with the world

Let the rules of it be bent to your whim

While you play with power do not become lost in the game

You can only lose by losing yourself

Do not be lost in the maze of your own creation

Such an action is a fate worse than death

Do not risk sinking beneath the waves of your own mind

It is not worth the effort of your curiosity

Perhaps that is why we throw ourselves into each other

To escape our thoughts so we may not fall beneath the waves

But entrapment is always waiting for you...

After all the music, the sights, the sound...

The shopping, the displays, the speeches...

The flawed human connection...

You are just as trapped as I
